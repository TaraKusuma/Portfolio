ERamen

ERamen is the oldest ramen shop in the world, established in 1910 and brought to Indonesia. For a long time, since its appearance, ERamen wanted to provide a new atmosphere for the customer. On this website ERamen shows the new and best seller products, a promotion banner, a list of ramen, side dishes, and drinks, and also offers products with discounted prices, ERamen also provides information about the company and the history of the company throughout the years.

This website was made as a Final Project for the Human-Computer Interaction Laboratorium Subject, using several programming languages.

Gesture Mind

Gesture Mind is a website platform for deaf people, converting a hand sign gesture to a subtitle text. Using AI, we are able to convert hand signs using camera detection to text that will be displayed on the monitor.

This project is a Final Project for the Artificial Intelligence subject, by implementing AI with real-life problems and solving them. This project was made by a group consisting of 5 people.

RM

Examines the link between social media use and ADHD symptoms by analyzing survey data from active social media users. The survey, rated on a Likert scale, assesses social media usage hours and ADHD-related symptoms. Using classification methods, the study evaluates models to predict ADHD likelihood based on social media activity.

This project was done for the Research Methodology subject, and supervised by the lecturer. Made in a group consisting of 2 people, we were able to identify and show the result of our research.

PeduliDisabilitas

Peduli Disabilitas is a marketplace platform designed specifically to empower people with disabilities through the industry. This platform serves as a space for them to showcase their skills, talents, and products to a broader audience. On Peduli Disabilitas, products crafted by people with disabilities can be sold directly to the public. Peduli Disabilitas acts as a bridge between talented individuals with disabilities in various fields and a community eager to promote inclusivity and equal opportunity.

This website is created as a Final Project for the Entrepreneurship: Market Validation subject. This project was made by a group consisting of 5 people.
